## Recommended Search Strategy

1. **NHM Wien direct browsing** (highest yield): Navigate objekte.nhm-wien.ac.at by collection category. Also download the Winserion demo for 1,300 Hallstatt cemetery images.
2. **German-language museum database searches**: Use German terms from the CLAUDE.md shared reference section. Austrian/German museum databases are far better indexed in German.
3. **Slovenian-language NMS searches**: Search nms.si in Slovenian for Dolenjska group material.
4. **Prometheus Bildarchiv** (requires institutional access): Search for Hallstatt, Situlenkunst, Hallstattkultur, Gürtelblech, Kahnfibel.
5. **Direct museum contact**: For Soproni Múzeum, Keltenmuseum Hallein, NMS Ljubljana, Landesmuseum Stuttgart — email requesting image access or digital collection links.
6. **ZOBODAT.at**: Austrian zoological-botanical database hosts many historical archaeological publications as PDFs with photographic plates.
