# Hallstatt Culture Research Project

A comprehensive archaeological reference corpus on the Hallstatt culture (Ha C–D, ~800–450 BC), covering the Eastern and Western Hallstatt zones from the Salzkammergut to the Heuneburg, from Stična to Vix.

## Sections

### [Research Collection](hallstatt_research/00_INDEX.html)
11 domain files (~49,500 words) covering chronology, salt mining, metallurgy, burial practices, elite seats, material culture, situla art, trade networks, settlement patterns, social organisation, and the La Tène transition.

### [Visual References](visual_references/00_INDEX.html)
17 domain files with 486 annotated links to museum databases, academic image archives, and excavation documentation — organised by attire/costume (A1–A8) and tools/equipment (B1–B9).

### [Prompt Generation](nano_banana_pro/00_INDEX.html)
20 archaeologically sourced Nano Banana Pro prompt suites for Hallstatt Celtic figure types, each with investigation reports, reference image guides, and copy-paste-ready prompts. Organised by period, status, gender, function, and regional variant.
